About Us

R3DLINE is all about BMW E30s.

[From wikipedia] The BMW E30 is an entry-level luxury car which was produced by BMW from 1982 to 1994. The initial models used the coupe (two-door sedan) body style. Four-door sedan models were introduced in 1983, convertibles were introduced in 1985 and estate ("Touring") models were introduced in 1987. The E36 replaced the E30 coupe models in 1990, with other E30 body styles being phased out of production until the final E30 model, a Touring, was produced in 1994.

R3DLINE provides a site where E30 enthusiasts can share pictures and advice so that more of these cars will stay on the road.
